About Me: Zabrina Lagamayo

Web Developer

I am analytical and inquisitive by nature which has led me to the wonderful path of Web Development. While working as a Market Researcher, I taught myself how to code VBA to improve work efficiencies. I then tried to teach myself JavaScript, Python and also dabbled a bit in trying to create a mechanical arm in my spare time but I quickly realised I needed to learn the fundamentals of programming and so I joined General Assembly.

I have always liked the idea of creating. Since undergoing the Web Development Immersive Course in General Assembly, I have come to appreciate the ability to create even more. Being challenged, overcoming the challenge and seeing the product of hardwork gives me a great sense of achievement. I would love to work for a company that nurtures growth and learning.
